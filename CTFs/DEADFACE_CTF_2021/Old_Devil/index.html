    Give me the flag!

    To get the flag just use Adrammelech as the answer to the program question: 

    ┌──(kali㉿kali)-[~/Downloads]
└─$ ./demon            

Luciafer v1.0
Say the demon's name to gain access to the secret.
Enter the demon's name: Adrammelech

You are correct.
